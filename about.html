About ChampCSS

Not convinced? Read more about the mission behind ChampCSS, and what problems that this framework attempts to solve.

Life Before ChampCSS

When you start a new website, there is a lot of decisions that need to be made. What typographic setup are you going to use? What padding are you going to use on your sections, how will you be making all this content responsive? What colour palette are you using? How are you going to tokenise it so that it’s useful for you? What about a reset? I have to figure out the exact recipe to nuke out all the browser agent styles. On and on we go. Before you know it, you are spending hours setting up your project before you even start laying out content on a page, and crafting your layout.

What ChampCSS Solves

With ChampCSS, I have done all the frameworking and tedious setup for you. This means that you are able to dive right into producing your web layouts without the need to configure anything beforehand. If you want to customize the framework, you can easily do that. ChampCSS is built with maintainability and scalability in mind.

How would You Use ChampCSS?

For Quick Labs

For times when you are just playing around in a lab environment, and you don’t want to worry about creating a system, you can just plug into ChampCSS, and it will take care of the rest.

For Full Website Builds

When you are building out full websites, ChampCSS is a no-brainer. Everything is already frameworked for you, so you can dive into the web production phase without any delay.
