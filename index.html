Outstanding Features

Flexible Color Management

ChampCSS makes it a breeze to manage your colour palettes. With partials automatically generated for you, you can easily update your colour, and all the variants automatically update across your entire site.

Fluid Typography and Spacing

With fluid type and spacing values, these automatically adapt based on the user’s device sizes. No more writing media queries for everything, it is all taken care of for you.

Semantic Variables

Semantic Tokens make it really easy to retheme a site, because you know what tokens control what, and you will feel really confident updating, and modifying your site’s look and feel far off into the future.
